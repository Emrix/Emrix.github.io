Experience at Outside The Box

Outside the Box is a new company in Provo, UT. They specialize in mobile, automatically resetting escape rooms. Information about their first escape room may be found on tempusescapes.com.

Embedded Systems Engineer

I started working at Outside the Box in September of 2023 as an Embedded Systems Engineer and continue to work there today. I thoroughly enjoy my role in bringing together a space themed escape room.

  • Debugging - The initial reason why I was brought on to Outside the Box was because of my skills in debugging embedded systems.

  • Board Design - Later on in the project, I will be using my skills and experience in designing circuit boards to assist in mass producing more escape room containers.

I've enjoyed every moment at Outside the Box, as I've gotten to stretch my skills, learn a lot, and I look forward to continuing to bring to pass the next generation of escape room technologies.
